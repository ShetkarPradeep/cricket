What is Cricket?

Cricket is a bat-and-a ball game played between two teams of eleven players each on a cricket field. At the center of the field is a rectangular 22-yard-long pitch with a wicket (a set of three wooden stumps) at each end. The game is popular in many countries, particularly in India, England, Australia, and South Africa.

Cricket Facts

Cricket is often referred to as the "gentleman's game."

The Cricket World Cup is held every four years.
